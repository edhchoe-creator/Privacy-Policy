Privacy Policy for CountDownReady+

Effective Date: 11/24/2025

CountDownReady+ ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights as a user of the app.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
CountDownReady+ does not require you to create an account or provide personal information. The only time we receive personal data is if you contact us directly by email.

Usage Data
CountDownReady+ does not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data. The App does not require accounts, registration, or any form of identification. The only information received is if you voluntarily contact support via email.

Third-Party Services
CountDownReady does not integrate with third-party tracking SDKs or advertising networks.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Respond to user-initiated support emails.
  • Debug technical issues and maintain stability.

3. Notification Permissions

The App may ask for permission to send notifications such as countdown reminders. Notification permissions are optional and handled fully by iOS. The App never collects or shares your notification data.

4. Sharing Your Information

CountDownReady+ does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party. Information is only shared if required by law.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request deletion of any information you have voluntarily sent to us.
  • Opt out of notifications at any time in your iPhone settings.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised Effective Date.
