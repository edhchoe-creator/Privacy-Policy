Privacy Policy for CountDownReady+

CountDownReady+ does not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data. The App does not require accounts, registration, or any form of identification. The only information received is if you voluntarily contact support via email.

Since no personal or usage data is collected, none is used for any purpose. Any support correspondence is handled solely to provide assistance.

Notifications
The App may request permission to send notifications (e.g., countdown reminders), but this is managed entirely by your device settings. No notification data is collected or shared.

Third-Party Services
CountDownReady+ does not integrate any third-party trackers, advertising networks, or analytics services.

Data Sharing
No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Information is only disclosed if required by law.

Your Rights
